About

Xinyuan is a graduate student specializing in Consumer Behavior at the Stephen M. Ross School of Business, University of Michigan. Her research interests encompass Consumer Journey and Identity, Artificial Intelligence, Anthropomorphism, and Luxury Consumption. Currently, her work focuses on exploring how "travel companions" contribute to enriching the customer journey beyond merely influencing outcomes. She also investigates the dynamics of customer journeys in joint decision-making contexts, employing a multidisciplinary approach that integrates diverse theories and methods to analyze social influences.
